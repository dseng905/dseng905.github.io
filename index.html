About Me

Hi! My name is Sovanarung Seng. You can call me Davin. I am an engineer who is interested in electronics and software development. I recently graduated from University of California, San Diego with a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering.

The world is big and diverse. So I tend to be a curious and open-minded person and always want to learn more especially outside of my specialty. Outside of engineering, I enjoy playing videogames, hiking, and listening to 80's New Wave and indie music!
