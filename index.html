About Me

Hi! My name is Sovanarung Seng. You can call me Davin. I am a software engineer who is interested in web and mobile app development. I graduated from University of California, San Diego with a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering in June 2019.

Throughout my time in college, I have done several projects including an EMG videogame controller and an autononmous RC car where I programmed in C++ and Python! While working on these projects, I became more interested in the field of software engineering, and so I taught myself web and mobile app development. I am skilled in JavaScript, React.js, Node.js, SQL, and NoSQL for full-stack web development and use Flutter and Dart for mobile app development.

Outside of engineering, I enjoy playing videogames, hiking, and listening to 80's New Wave and indie music! The world is big and diverse. So I tend to be a curious and open-minded person and always want to learn more especially outside of my specialty.

NewsTyper

NewsTyper is a web app that allows you practice your typing skills by typing news articles! Get the latest news happening in the world, politics, and more from the Guardian, a reputable British newspaper.

  • Front-end: TypeScript, React.js, React Router, React Context API, styled-components
  • Back-end: Node.js, Express.js, PostgresSQL, Prisma, passport.js
